## History

**Founded:** 1958
**Founders:** Brothers Carl and Leo Hobbs

**Origin Story:**
While shoveling potatoes on a cold morning, the Hobbs brothers "decided there must be a better way to handle potatoes." They built their first potato scooper from spare shop parts, establishing the foundation for over 60 years of agricultural equipment innovation.

**2003:** Joined the **Grimme Group** (German parent company led by Franz Grimme)
- Sister companies: ASA-Lift, Ricon, Internorm
- **All manufacturing remains exclusively in the United States** at the Blackfoot, Idaho facility
